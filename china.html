Important Update 重要更新

China joined the Hague Apostille Convention on November 7, 2023. The Chinese Embassy and Consulates in the US no longer process document authentication. All US documents intended for use in China now require an apostille — and CNA is here to handle it seamlessly. 中国于2023年11月7日加入海牙认证公约。中国驻美大使馆及领事馆已停止办理文件认证，所有在华使用的美国文件现须办理认证手续。
