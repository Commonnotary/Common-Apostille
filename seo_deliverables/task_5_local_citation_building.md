# Task 5: Local Citation Building

This document provides a master list of every citation source Common Notary Apostille (CNA) must appear on for local SEO dominance in DC, Northern Virginia, Richmond, and Southwest Virginia.

## General Business Directories

| Directory | URL | Submission Steps |
|---|---|---|
| Google Business Profile | [google.com/business](https://google.com/business) | 1. Sign in with a Google account. 2. Search for your business name and address. 3. Claim the existing profile or create a new one. 4. Verify your business via mail, phone, or email. 5. Complete all sections of the profile with accurate information. |
| Yelp | [biz.yelp.com](https://biz.yelp.com) | 1. Search for your business on Yelp. 2. Click the "Claim this Business" button. 3. Create a business account. 4. Verify your business via phone. 5. Complete your profile with photos, business hours, and other details. |
| Bing Places | [bingplaces.com](https://www.bingplaces.com/) | 1. Sign in with a Microsoft account. 2. Search for your business. 3. Claim the existing listing or create a new one. 4. Verify your business. 5. Complete your profile. |
| Apple Maps | [mapsconnect.apple.com](https://mapsconnect.apple.com/) | 1. Sign in with an Apple ID. 2. Search for your business. 3. Add or update your business information. 4. Verify your business. |
| Facebook | [facebook.com/business](https://www.facebook.com/business) | 1. Create a Facebook Page for your business. 2. Select "Local Business or Place." 3. Fill in your business information. 4. Complete your Page with a profile picture, cover photo, and other details. |
| LinkedIn | [linkedin.com/company/setup/new/](https://www.linkedin.com/company/setup/new/) | 1. Create a LinkedIn Company Page. 2. Fill in your company details. 3. Complete your profile with a logo, cover image, and description. |

## Government Vendor Portals

| Directory | URL | Submission Steps |
|---|---|---|
| Virginia eVA | [eva.virginia.gov](https://eva.virginia.gov/) | 1. Register as a new vendor. 2. Complete the company profile and provide details about the services offered. 3. Certify as a small, woman-owned, or minority-owned business (SWaM) if applicable. |
| DC Office of Contracting and Procurement (OCP) | [ocp.dc.gov](https://ocp.dc.gov/service/doing-business-district) | 1. Register in the DC Vendor Portal. 2. Complete the necessary forms and certifications. |
| Maryland eMMA | [emma.maryland.gov](https://emma.maryland.gov/) | 1. Register as a new vendor on the eMaryland Marketplace Advantage (eMMA). 2. Complete the company profile and commodity codes. |
