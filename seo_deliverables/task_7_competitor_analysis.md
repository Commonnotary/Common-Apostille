# Task 7: Competitor Analysis

This document analyzes the top 10 competitors of Common Notary Apostille (CNA) in the DC, Northern Virginia, and Southwest Virginia apostille markets. For each competitor, we provide their website URL, Google rating, services offered, pricing (if visible), SEO strengths and weaknesses, and how CNA can differentiate and outrank them.

## Competitor 1: DC Mobile Notary

*   **Website:** [dcmobilenotary.com](https://www.dcmobilenotary.com/)
*   **Google Rating:** 4.9 (653 reviews)
*   **Services Offered:** Apostille services, mobile notary, translation services, remote/online notary, embassy legalization, real estate closing, I-9 employment verification.
*   **Pricing:** Not publicly listed; quote-based.
*   **SEO Strengths:**
    *   Very high review count (653 reviews), which is a major trust signal for both Google and AI search engines.
    *   Well-structured website with clear service pages and a blog.
    *   Strong domain authority due to age and backlinks.
    *   Active on social media (Yelp, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook).
*   **SEO Weaknesses:**
    *   Focuses primarily on volume, which may dilute the premium positioning.
    *   Website content is broad and not as deeply specialized in specific niches (e.g., estate planning, digital depositions).
*   **CNA Differentiation Strategy:** CNA can differentiate by emphasizing its founder-verified precision, its specialization in complex legal documents (estate planning, digital depositions), and its premium positioning. CNA should also focus on building its review count to compete with DC Mobile Notary's strong social proof.
