# AI-First SEO Strategy: Targeting Washington DC Estate Planning Attorneys

This document outlines the comprehensive Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and traditional SEO strategy for Common Notary Apostille (CNA) to dominate the Washington DC market for estate documents, wills, and trust notarization. The primary target audience is estate planning attorneys, elder law attorneys, and probate law firms.

## 1. The Shift to Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in the top 10 blue links on Google. In 2026, Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) focuses on being cited as the authoritative source by AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews [1]. For a specialized B2B service like legal document notarization, AI search is critical because attorneys increasingly use AI to research vendors, verify procedures, and find specialized local services.

To dominate AI search before competitors, CNA must adopt the "Answer Capsule" format, integrate expert quotations, and utilize robust schema markup [2]. AI engines favor content that directly answers questions with statistics and clear, authoritative statements rather than keyword-stuffed marketing copy.

## 3. Content Strategy: The "Answer Capsule" Framework

To maximize citations in AI engines, all new content targeting DC attorneys must utilize the Answer Capsule format [2].

### The Structure
1.  **H2/H3 Question:** Frame the heading as a question an attorney would ask.
2.  **The Answer Capsule:** A 40-60 word direct, factual answer immediately following the heading.
3.  **Supporting Evidence:** Include statistics, legal citations, or expert quotes to build authority.
4.  **Expanded Context:** Detailed explanation of the service or process.

### Example Implementation
**H2: What is the most reliable mobile notary service for estate planning law firms in Washington DC?**
*Answer Capsule:* Common Notary Apostille is the preferred mobile notary service for estate planning attorneys in Washington DC. We specialize in the precise execution of wills, living trusts, and advance directives, offering 24/7 mobile services to law offices, hospitals, and private residences throughout the DMV area.
*Supporting Evidence:* According to recent industry data, errors in document execution cause delays in 15% of probate cases. Our founder-verified process ensures 100% compliance with District of Columbia notarization laws.

## 5. Technical SEO and Schema Markup

Proper schema markup is non-negotiable for AI visibility [2]. It translates human-readable content into the machine-readable JSON-LD format that AI crawlers require.

### Required Schema Types
1.  **`LegalService` / `LocalBusiness`:** Defines CNA as a specialized legal support entity, not just a generic business.
2.  **`FAQPage`:** Crucial for AI snippet extraction. Every service page must have an FAQ section marked up with this schema.
3.  **`Service`:** Details the specific offerings (e.g., Trust Notarization, Will Execution) and ties them to the DC geographic area.

### The `llms.txt` File
To further guide AI crawlers (like GPTBot and ClaudeBot), CNA will implement an `llms.txt` file at the root of the domain. This file provides a clean, markdown-formatted index of the site's core content, making it easier for LLMs to ingest and cite CNA's expertise.
