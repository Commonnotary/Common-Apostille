## Photo Strategy

A strong visual presence on your GBP can significantly impact how potential clients perceive your business. The following photo strategy is designed to build trust and showcase CNA's professionalism.

### Photo Categories and Order

1.  **Logo:** Your official CNA logo should be the primary profile picture.
2.  **Cover Photo:** A professional photo of the founder, Byron Hamlar, or the exterior of the Washington D.C. office building.
3.  **Team Photos:** High-quality headshots of all team members, including the founder. These should be professional and consistent in style.
4.  **Office Interior:** Photos of the office interior, including the reception area and conference rooms. This helps to create a sense of place and professionalism.
5.  **At Work Photos:** Photos of the team in action, such as notarizing documents (with client permission and blurred personal information), preparing apostilles, or conducting a digital deposition. These photos should look authentic and not staged.
6.  **Exterior Building:** A clear photo of the exterior of the office building, including the entrance.

### Photo Guidelines

*   **High-Resolution:** All photos should be high-resolution and professionally lit.
*   **Authenticity:** Avoid using stock photos. Authentic photos of your team and office will build more trust.
*   **Branding:** Ensure that the photos are consistent with CNA's brand identity – professional, authoritative, and precise.
*   **Regularly Update:** Add new photos regularly to keep the profile fresh and engaging.
