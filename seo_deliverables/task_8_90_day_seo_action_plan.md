# Task 8: 90-Day SEO Action Plan

This document outlines a week-by-week, executable 90-day SEO action plan for Common Notary Apostille (CNA) to achieve its search visibility goals.

## Month 1: Foundational Setup & On-Page SEO

### Week 1: Website & GBP Implementation

*   **Action:** Implement the recommended website architecture, including URL structure, page creation, and meta tags.
*   **Action:** Fully optimize the Google Business Profile, including implementing the photo strategy and the first GBP description variation.
*   **Action:** Begin the citation building process by submitting CNA to the top 10 general business directories.
*   **Milestone:** All core website pages are live and optimized. GBP is fully optimized.

### Week 2: Content Deployment & Schema Markup

*   **Action:** Publish the homepage copy and the five country-specific apostille landing pages.
*   **Action:** Implement the schema markup as outlined in the GEO strategy.
*   **Action:** Publish the first two blog posts from the content calendar.
*   **Action:** Post the first two weekly GBP posts.
*   **Milestone:** All initial website content is live. Schema markup is implemented. Blog and GBP are active.

### Week 3: Citation Building & Review Generation

*   **Action:** Continue the citation building process by submitting CNA to the legal-specific and notary-specific directories.
*   **Action:** Implement the review generation system and begin outreach to past clients.
*   **Action:** Publish the next two blog posts.
*   **Action:** Post the next two weekly GBP posts.
*   **Milestone:** Citation building is well underway. Review generation system is active.

### Week 4: Local SEO & Content Expansion

*   **Action:** Create and publish the 12 location-specific landing pages.
*   **Action:** Continue citation building with government vendor portals and Chamber of Commerce listings.
*   **Action:** Publish the next two blog posts.
*   **Action:** Post the next two weekly GBP posts.
*   **Milestone:** All location pages are live. Citation building is nearing completion.
## Month 2: Content Velocity & Authority Building

### Week 5-8: Consistent Content & Link Building

*   **Action:** Publish one new blog post per week.
*   **Action:** Post one new GBP post per week.
*   **Action:** Begin a targeted link-building campaign, focusing on legal blogs, industry publications, and local news outlets.
*   **Action:** Distribute the press release to relevant media outlets.
*   **Action:** Monitor keyword rankings and website traffic, making adjustments to the content strategy as needed.
*   **Milestone:** A consistent flow of new content is established. Link-building campaign is active. Press release is distributed.
## Month 3: Optimization & Expansion

### Week 9-12: Performance Analysis & Optimization

*   **Action:** Conduct a comprehensive analysis of website performance, including keyword rankings, organic traffic, and conversion rates.
*   **Action:** A/B test the different GBP description variations to identify the most effective messaging.
*   **Action:** Identify new content opportunities based on performance data and keyword research.
*   **Action:** Continue the link-building campaign, focusing on high-authority domains.
*   **Action:** Continue publishing one new blog post and one new GBP post per week.
*   **Milestone:** A data-driven approach to SEO is established. The website and GBP are continuously optimized for performance.
