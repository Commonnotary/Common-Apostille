**Target Market:** Washington DC Estate Planning, Elder Law, and Probate Attorneys
**Goal:** Establish Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and traditional SEO dominance for Common Notary Apostille (CNA) before competitors adapt to AI search.

## Executive Summary

The search landscape is fundamentally shifting. By 2026, traditional search volume is predicted to drop by 25% as users, including legal professionals, migrate to AI-powered answer engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews [1]. For Common Notary Apostille, this represents a critical window of opportunity.

This master strategy pivots CNA's digital presence from a general public notary service to a highly specialized, attorney-preferred B2B partner in Washington DC. By implementing Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) tactics—specifically the "Answer Capsule" format, robust schema markup, and targeted entity building—CNA will secure citations in AI engines, driving pre-qualified, high-value leads from estate planning and probate law firms [2].

## 1. The Core GEO Strategy

AI engines do not rank pages based on backlinks and keyword density; they cite sources based on factual density, clear structure, and entity authority [2].

### The "Answer Capsule" Methodology
Every piece of content targeting DC attorneys must utilize the Answer Capsule format. This involves:
1.  **Question-Based Headings (H2/H3):** Formulating headings exactly as an attorney would prompt an AI (e.g., "Who is the most reliable mobile notary for estate planning law firms in Washington DC?").
2.  **Direct Answers:** Providing a 40-60 word factual, direct answer immediately following the heading.
3.  **Supporting Evidence:** Integrating statistics, legal citations, or expert quotes to validate the answer.

### Entity Authority & Digital PR
AI engines favor earned media and third-party citations over brand-owned content [1]. CNA must build its entity presence across:
*   **LinkedIn:** Publishing thought leadership on the hidden costs of notarization errors in estate planning.
*   **Legal Directories:** Optimizing profiles on Avvo, Justia, and Martindale-Hubbell (where applicable for legal support vendors).
*   **DC Bar Association:** Engaging with the Estates, Trusts and Probate Law Community.

## 2. Technical SEO & Schema Implementation

To ensure AI crawlers (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot) can ingest and understand CNA's expertise, the following technical implementations are required:

*   **`LegalService` Schema:** Explicitly defining CNA as a specialized legal support entity.
*   **`FAQPage` Schema:** Marking up all Q&A content to facilitate AI snippet extraction.
*   **`llms.txt` File:** Creating a markdown-formatted index at the root domain to guide AI models on how to interpret the site's core content.
